What is $SOS?

$SOS is a Solana-native memecoin built around a guardian character — made to live inside the Solana culture loop.

Years later, a new world emerged. Chaotic. Viral. Unstoppable. Out of this chaos, the fastest chain in crypto was born: Solana.

Not created by marketing. Not chosen by chance. Aligned by destiny.

The guardian of balance did not disappear — she went on-chain to become the guardian of memecoins and culture that Solana needs now more than ever.
